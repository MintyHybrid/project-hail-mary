10.3 Software and R packages

All analyses were carried out in R (R Core Team 2026) with Quarto. The work relies heavily on the rOpenSci ecosystem — rentrez for NCBI Entrez access (Winter 2025), rotl for the Open Tree of Life (Michonneau et al. 2026), rphylopic for silhouettes (Gearty and Jones 2025), and crul for HTTP (Chamberlain 2025) — alongside the Bioconductor sequence/tree stack (Biostrings (Pagès et al. 2026), DECIPHER (Wright 2026), msa (Bonatesta et al. 2026), ggtree/treeio (Yu et al. 2026; Yu 2026)), the phylogenetics packages ape (Paradis et al. 2024) and phytools (Revell 2025), codon/sequence tools seqinr (Charif and Lobry 2026) and ggseqlogo (Wagih 2025), the network packages igraph (Csárdi et al. 2026), ggraph (Pedersen 2025) and visNetwork (Almende B.V. and Contributors and Thieurmel 2025), interactive widgets plotly (Sievert et al. 2026), DT (Xie et al. 2025) and r3dmol (Su and Johnston 2021), metacoder (Foster 2026), and the tidyverse (Wickham et al. 2019). Analysis functions specific to this project are collected in the local potytools package (Muedsam 2026). Every package used, with its version, is listed in the session information below, and all software citations are collected in the complete bibliography.

## Software and R packages

All analyses were carried out in **R** [@R-base] with **Quarto**. The work relies
heavily on the **rOpenSci** ecosystem — `rentrez` for NCBI Entrez access
[@R-rentrez], `rotl` for the Open Tree of Life [@R-rotl], `rphylopic` for
silhouettes [@R-rphylopic], and `crul` for HTTP [@R-crul] — alongside the
Bioconductor sequence/tree stack (`Biostrings` [@R-Biostrings], `DECIPHER`
[@R-DECIPHER], `msa` [@R-msa], `ggtree`/`treeio` [@R-ggtree; @R-treeio]), the
phylogenetics packages `ape` [@R-ape] and `phytools` [@R-phytools], codon/sequence
tools `seqinr` [@R-seqinr] and `ggseqlogo` [@R-ggseqlogo], the network packages
`igraph` [@R-igraph], `ggraph` [@R-ggraph] and `visNetwork` [@R-visNetwork],
interactive widgets `plotly` [@R-plotly], `DT` [@R-DT] and `r3dmol` [@R-r3dmol],
`metacoder` [@R-metacoder], and the `tidyverse` [@R-tidyverse]. Analysis functions
specific to this project are collected in the local **`potytools`** package
[@R-potytools]. **Every package used, with its version, is listed in the session
information below, and all software citations are collected in the complete
bibliography.**
